Extension Settings

×

To access the extension settings, please use one of the following methods based on your browser:

C Chrome

  1. Click the extensions icon (puzzle piece) in the browser toolbar
  2. Find "JIRA URL Wizard" in the list
  3. Click the three dots (⋮) next to the extension
  4. Select "Options" or "Extension options"

F Firefox

  1. Click the menu button (≡) in the top right
  2. Select "Add-ons and themes"
  3. Click on "Extensions" in the sidebar
  4. Find "JIRA URL Wizard" and click the three dots (⋮)
  5. Select "Options" or "Preferences"

E Edge

  1. Click the extensions icon (puzzle piece) in the browser toolbar
  2. Find "JIRA URL Wizard" in the list
  3. Click the three dots (⋮) next to the extension
  4. Select "Manage extension"
  5. Scroll down and click on "Extension options"

D Direct Access

  1. Click the JIRA URL Wizard icon in your browser toolbar
  2. When the popup opens, click on "Manage Extension Settings"
