JIRA URL Wizard Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2024

1. Introduction

JIRA URL Wizard ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension. We take your privacy seriously and are transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

JIRA URL Wizard collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

2.1 Configuration Data

  • JIRA environment configurations (URLs, names, authentication details)
  • Custom environment settings and preferences
  • User interface preferences (theme, layout, etc.)

2.2 Ticket Data

  • Saved ticket information (ticket IDs, descriptions)
  • Ticket categorization and organization
  • Quick access ticket lists

2.3 Usage Data

  • Extension settings and preferences
  • Last accessed environments
  • User-defined shortcuts and hotkeys

Important: All data is stored locally in your browser's storage and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. Detailed Permission Usage

JIRA URL Wizard requires the following permissions to function properly. Here's a detailed explanation of why each permission is needed:

storage

Purpose: To save your configurations and preferences locally

Usage: This permission allows the extension to:

  • Save your JIRA environment configurations
  • Store your ticket information
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Maintain your quick access lists

Data Handling: All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API. No data is sent to external servers.

tabs

Purpose: To detect and interact with JIRA tabs

Usage: This permission enables the extension to:

  • Detect when you're on a JIRA page
  • Extract ticket information from the current tab
  • Generate appropriate URLs for different environments
  • Switch between JIRA instances

Data Handling: The extension only reads the URL and title of tabs that are JIRA-related. No other tab information is accessed or stored.

activeTab

Purpose: To access the current tab's URL when generating ticket links

Usage: This permission allows the extension to:

  • Read the current tab's URL when you click the extension icon
  • Generate appropriate ticket links based on the current context
  • Provide quick access to related tickets

Data Handling: The extension only accesses the active tab when you explicitly interact with it. No background tab access occurs.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data collected by JIRA URL Wizard is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We implement the following security measures:

  • All data is encrypted using Chrome's built-in storage encryption
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No tracking or analytics services are used
  • Regular security audits of the codebase
  • Secure handling of sensitive information

5. Your Rights and Controls

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access your stored data through the extension's options page
  • Delete your stored data at any time
  • Export your configurations for backup
  • Import configurations from other devices
  • Reset all settings to default

6. Data Retention

Your data is stored locally on your device until you:

  • Explicitly delete it through the extension's options
  • Uninstall the extension
  • Clear your browser data
