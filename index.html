JIRA URL Wizard Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2024

1. Introduction

JIRA URL Wizard ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

JIRA URL Wizard collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • JIRA environment configurations (URLs, names)
  • Saved ticket information
  • User preferences and settings

All data is stored locally in your browser's storage and is not transmitted to any external servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Generating JIRA ticket URLs
  • Managing your saved environments
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Improving the extension's functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

All data collected by JIRA URL Wizard is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not:

  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use tracking or analytics services

5. Permissions

JIRA URL Wizard requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your configurations and preferences locally
  • tabs: To detect and interact with JIRA tabs
  • activeTab: To access the current tab's URL when generating ticket links

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through the extension's options page
  • Delete your stored data at any time
  • Export your configurations

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
